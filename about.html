About Me

I’m Kalaivanan, an Accessibility Specialist with over 2 years of professional experience ensuring that digital content meets WCAG and PDF/UA standards. I’ve successfully collaborated with 15+ clients, delivering high-quality accessible PDFs, forms, and compliance reports that make digital information usable for everyone.
